Our Story

Born from a simple need to move files between Google accounts, GDrive Transfer has evolved into the world's most trusted file migration platform.

It started in 2023 when our founder faced the challenge of migrating thousands of files from a personal Google account to a new business account. The manual process was tedious, time-consuming, and error-prone. There had to be a better way.

After weeks of research and development, the first version of GDrive Transfer was born. What began as a personal solution quickly attracted attention from others facing the same challenge. Today, we've helped transfer over 2.5 million files for more than 50,000 users worldwide.
