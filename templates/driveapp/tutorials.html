Complete Step-by-Step Guide

Follow this comprehensive tutorial to complete your first transfer

1

Create an Account & Sign In

Start by registering for a GDrive Transfer account or signing in if you already have one. This helps us track your transfers and provide better support.

Screenshot: Registration page
Pro Tips:
  • Use the same email you use for your Google accounts for easier management
  • Choose a strong password to keep your transfer history secure
2

Connect Your Source Google Drive

Click "Connect Source Account" and sign in to the Google account that contains the files you want to transfer. You'll be redirected to Google's secure authentication page.

Screenshot: OAuth authentication
Pro Tips:
  • Make sure you're signed out of other Google accounts to avoid confusion
  • Review the permissions carefully - we only request what's necessary
3

Connect Your Destination Google Drive

After successfully connecting your source account, click "Connect Destination Account" and sign in to the Google account where you want to transfer the files.

Screenshot: Destination connection
Pro Tips:
  • This can be a personal or business Google account
  • Ensure the destination account has enough storage space
4

Select Folders to Transfer

Choose which folders you want to transfer from the source drive and where to place them in the destination drive. You can select the root folder to transfer everything.

Screenshot: Folder selection interface
Pro Tips:
  • Start with smaller folders to test the process
  • Choose "Root Folder" to transfer your entire drive
5

Start Transfer & Monitor Progress

Click "Start Transfer" to begin the process. You'll be redirected to a status page where you can monitor the real-time progress of your transfer.

Screenshot: Transfer progress page
Pro Tips:
  • Keep the browser tab open for faster progress updates
  • You can safely close the tab - the transfer continues in the background
  • Check back periodically to monitor progress
