Our Cookie Policy

At GDrive Transfer, we use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience, analyze usage, and provide personalized features. This policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and how you can control them.

Quick Summary

Your Control

You can manage cookie preferences and opt out of non-essential cookies anytime.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. They contain information that helps websites remember your preferences, analyze traffic, and provide a better user experience.

Types of Storage Technologies

HTTP Cookies

Traditional cookies stored in your browser

Local Storage

Data stored locally in your browser

Session Storage

Temporary data for the current session

Browser Fingerprinting

Technical information about your browser

Why We Use Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies for several important purposes:

Authentication & Security

To keep you logged in securely and protect against unauthorized access. These cookies are essential for our service to function properly.

Service Functionality

To remember your preferences, maintain your session state, and provide core features like transfer monitoring and progress tracking.

Analytics & Improvement

To understand how our service is used, identify issues, and improve performance. This helps us make GDrive Transfer better for everyone.

Personalization

To remember your preferences like theme settings, dashboard layouts, and other customization options.

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

Required

These cookies are necessary for our website to function and cannot be switched off. They're usually set in response to actions you take, such as logging in or filling out forms.

Examples:
  • Authentication tokens
  • Session management
  • Security cookies
  • Load balancing

Functional Cookies

Optional

These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalization. They may be set by us or third-party providers whose services we use.

Examples:
  • Theme preferences (dark/light mode)
  • Language settings
  • Dashboard customization
  • User interface preferences

Analytics Cookies

Optional

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously.

Examples:
  • Page views and navigation
  • Feature usage statistics
  • Performance metrics
  • Error tracking

Marketing Cookies

Optional

These cookies track your activity across websites to help us provide more relevant advertising and marketing content.

Examples:
  • Social media integration
  • Advertising preferences
  • Campaign tracking
  • Conversion measurement
