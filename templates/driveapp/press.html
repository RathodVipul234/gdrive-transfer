Company Overview

GDrive Transfer is the world's leading platform for secure Google Drive file migration, trusted by over 50,000 users worldwide to safely transfer millions of files between accounts.

Founded in 2023, GDrive Transfer was created to solve the complex challenge of migrating files between Google Drive accounts. What started as a personal solution has evolved into a comprehensive platform that prioritizes security, simplicity, and user experience.

Our mission is to make data migration accessible to everyone, from individual users switching personal accounts to enterprises managing large-scale transitions. We've built our reputation on zero data storage policies, bank-level security, and exceptional customer support.

Key Differentiators

  • Zero Data Storage: Files transfer directly between user accounts - we never store, access, or view your data
  • Bank-Level Security: End-to-end encryption with OAuth 2.0 authentication
  • Preserve Structure: Maintains exact folder hierarchy and file organization
  • Real-time Monitoring: Live progress tracking with detailed transfer logs
  • 100% Free: No hidden fees, premium tiers, or usage limits

Media Guidelines

Brand Usage Guidelines

  • Always use the most recent version of our logo
  • Maintain minimum clear space around the logo equal to the height of the "G" in GDrive
  • Do not alter, stretch, or modify the logo in any way
  • Use the appropriate logo version for your background (light/dark)
  • Our brand colors are #667eea (primary) and #f5576c (secondary)

Company Name Usage

  • Correct: "GDrive Transfer" (always capitalize both words)
  • Incorrect: "gdrive transfer", "GdriveTransfer", "G-Drive Transfer"
  • In headlines, you may use "GDrive Transfer" on first mention, then "the platform" or "the service"

Key Messaging

  • Focus on security, simplicity, and zero data storage
  • Emphasize our 99.9% success rate and reliability
  • Highlight that the service is completely free with no limitations
  • Mention our growth (50,000+ users, 2.5M+ files transferred)
